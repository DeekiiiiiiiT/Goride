Privacy Policy

Roam Rides & Roam Driver · Last updated June 02, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Sadiki Thomas, operating as Roam Enterprise ("we," "us," or "our"), collects, uses, shares, and protects information from users of the Roam Rides (Passenger) app and the Roam Driver partner app (collectively, the "Applications").

By downloading, installing, or using the Applications, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

To provide reliable rideshare services across Jamaica, we collect personal data from both passengers and drivers.

A. Information provided directly by you

  • Account information: Name, email address, mobile phone number, profile photo, and password created during account setup.
  • Driver-specific data: Government-issued identification, driver's license, vehicle registration, fitness certificate, background check documentation, and banking information for earnings payouts. Driver account creation requires photo uploads from your device's camera or gallery.
  • Trusted contacts & shared trips: Passengers may voluntarily provide contact information from their device to use the "Share Trip Status" feature with friends or family.
  • Payment information: Card or wallet details processed securely by our payment partners; we do not store full card numbers on our servers.

B. Information collected automatically

  • Location data: For passengers, we collect precise location when Roam Rides runs in the foreground for pickup selection and active trip tracking. For drivers, we collect precise location in the foreground and background (when the app is minimized or the screen is closed) to dispatch trip requests, show live routes to passengers, and calculate fares based on distance traveled.
  • In-app messages: Text sent between passengers and drivers during an active trip is processed and temporarily stored on our servers.
  • Device & push notification data: Device identifiers, operating system version, IP address, crash logs, and tokens used for push notifications.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Creating and maintaining passenger and driver accounts.
  • Matching passengers with nearby drivers.
  • Live GPS navigation and tracking from pickup to drop-off.
  • Processing digital fare payments, recording cash-based transactions, and issuing driver payouts.
  • Improving app stability, dispatch efficiency, and security.

3. Data Sharing and Service Providers

We do not sell user data. We share information only to operate the platform:

  • Passenger to driver: Pickup location, drop-off destination, first name, and profile photo.
  • Driver to passenger: First name, profile photo, vehicle make/model, license plate, current map location, and average star rating.
  • Supabase: Authentication, database, profile hosting, and SMS/OTP routing.
  • Google LLC: Maps, routing, and optional Google Sign-In.
  • Payment processors: Third-party providers we integrate with (e.g. WiPay, PayPal, or bank payment partners). Digital and cash trip records are retained for accounting.

Data may be processed on cloud servers outside Jamaica through infrastructure partners such as Supabase. By using the service, you consent to this transfer.

We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or government request, or to protect users, drivers, and platform safety.

4. Security and Data Retention

We use industry-standard encryption (HTTPS/TLS) to transfer sensitive data. We retain personal data while your account is active or as required for financial and regulatory record-keeping.

5. Access, Updates, and Data Deletion

You may update profile details in the app at any time.

Because in-app automated deletion is under development, you may request a copy of your data or permanent account deletion by emailing deekiiiiiii@gmail.com. After identity verification, we delete profile data, document uploads, and account histories from active databases within 30 days.

6. Children and Youth Privacy

The Applications are intended for users aged 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 13. Users aged 13–17 must not use the service without explicit parental or guardian consent.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Significant changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date or via an in-app notice.
