### **Part 1: Fluids & Specifications Reference**

*(For Mechanic Use)*

| Component | Recommended Spec | Capacity |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Engine Oil** | **0W-20** (Preferred) or 5W-30 | ~3.1 Liters (w/ filter) |
| **CVT Fluid** | **Toyota CVT Fluid FE** | ~2.5 - 3.0 Liters (Drain & Refill) |
| **Coolant** | Toyota Super Long Life (Pink) | ~4.5 Liters |
| **Brake Fluid** | DOT 3 or DOT 4 | As required |
| **Spark Plugs** | Iridium (NGK/Denso) | Gap: 1.0 - 1.1 mm |

### **Part 2: Service Checklist**

#### **A. Basic Service (Every 5,000 km)**

*Essential for engine health in heat/traffic.*

* [ ] **Replace Engine Oil:** Grade used: ___________
* [ ] **Replace Oil Filter**
* [ ] **Check Tire Pressures:** Set to ____ PSI (LF) ____ (RF) ____ (LR) ____ (RR)
* [ ] **Top Up Window Washer Fluid**
* [ ] **Check Coolant Level** (Reservoir)
* [ ] **Check Lights:** Headlights, Brake lights, Indicators, Reverse light.

#### **B. Intermediate Service (Every 10,000 km)**

*Includes all items in "Basic Service" plus:*

* [ ] **Rotate Tires:** Front to Back / Cross pattern.
* [ ] **Inspect Engine Air Filter:**
* [ ] Cleaned
* [ ] Replaced


* [ ] **Replace Cabin A/C Filter:** (Located behind glovebox).
* [ ] **Inspect Wiper Blades:** Replace if cracking or streaking.
* [ ] **Inspect Brake Pads:** _____% Life remaining.

#### **C. Major Service (Every 40,000 km)**

*Includes all items in "Intermediate Service" plus:*

* [ ] **Drain & Refill CVT Transmission Fluid:** (Use Toyota CVT FE only).
* [ ] **Flush & Replace Brake Fluid**
* [ ] **Inspect Drive/Serpentine Belt:** Check for glazing or cracks.
* [ ] **Inspect Suspension Bushings & Boots:** Check CV joints and shocks for leaks.

#### **D. Long-Term (Every 100,000 km)**

* [ ] **Replace Spark Plugs:** (Must use Iridium).
* [ ] **Flush Radiator Coolant:** (Super Long Life Pink).
