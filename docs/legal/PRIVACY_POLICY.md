# Privacy Policy for Roam Rides & Roam Driver

**Last Updated:** June 02, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Sadiki Thomas, operating as Roam Enterprise ("we," "us," or "our"), collects, uses, shares, and protects information from users of the Roam Rides (Passenger) app and the Roam Driver partner app (collectively, the "Applications").

By downloading, installing, or using the Applications, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

## 1. Information We Collect

To provide reliable rideshare services across Jamaica, we collect personal data from both passengers and drivers.

### A. Information Provided Directly by You

- **Account Information:** Name, email address, mobile phone number, profile photo, and password created during account setup.
- **Driver Specific Data:** Government-issued identification, driver's license, vehicle registration, fitness certificate, background check documentation, and banking information for earnings payouts. Driver account creation requires photo uploads from your device's camera or gallery.
- **Trusted Contacts & Shared Trips:** Passengers may voluntarily provide contact information from their device to utilize the "Share Trip Status" feature with friends or family.
- **Payment Information:** Card or wallet details processed securely by our payment partners; we do not store full card numbers on our servers.

### B. Information Collected Automatically Through App Usage

- **Location Data (Crucial Feature):**
  - *For Passengers:* We collect precise location data when the Roam Rides app runs in the foreground to facilitate pickup location selection and active trip tracking.
  - *For Drivers:* We collect precise location data through the Roam Driver app in the foreground and the background (when the app is minimized or the screen is closed). This background collection is mandatory to accurately dispatch nearby trip requests, display real-time map routes to passengers, and correctly calculate final trip fares based on distance traveled.
- **In-App Messages:** Text communications sent between passengers and drivers during an active trip are processed and temporarily stored on our servers to facilitate coordination.
- **Device & Push Notification Data:** Mobile device ID, operating system version, IP address, system crash logs, and device tokens required to transmit push notifications.

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data for operational purposes, including:

- Creating and maintaining passenger and driver accounts.
- Matching passengers with nearby drivers based on proximity.
- Providing live GPS navigation and tracking from pickup to drop-off.
- Processing digital fare payments, recording cash-based transactions, and issuing payouts to drivers.
- Improving app stability, dispatch efficiency, and internal system security.

## 3. Data Sharing and Service Providers

We do not sell user data to third parties. We share information only to facilitate core platform operations:

### A. Core Operational Sharing

- **Passenger to Driver:** The driver sees your pickup location, drop-off destination, first name, and profile photo.
- **Driver to Passenger:** The passenger sees your first name, profile photo, vehicle make/model, license plate number, current map location, and average star rating.

### B. Third-Party Service Providers

We utilize trusted third-party cloud infrastructure and services to power the Applications. These providers process your data strictly under our instruction:

- **Supabase:** Used for user authentication, secure database management, user profile hosting, and SMS authentication/OTP routing.
- **Google LLC:** Powering Google Maps API geolocation, mapping paths, and optional Google Sign-In protocols.
- **Payment Processors:** Digital payments may be processed by third-party payment providers we integrate with (e.g. WiPay, PayPal, or bank payment partners). Transaction records for both digital and cash-based trips are preserved for accounting verification.

### C. Cross-Border Data Transfers

The data we collect through our infrastructure partners (such as Supabase) may be processed and stored on cloud servers located outside of Jamaica. By using the service, you consent to this transfer.

### D. Legal Disclosures

We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or government request, or to protect users, drivers, and platform safety.

## 4. Security and Data Retention

We utilize industry-standard encryption protocols (HTTPS/TLS) to safely transfer your sensitive data. We retain your personal data for as long as your account remains active or as required by financial regulations to track transaction histories.

## 5. Access, Updates, and Data Deletion

Users retain total control over their data footprint. You may update your profile details directly in the app at any time.

Because in-app automated deletion pathways are currently under development, you may request a copy of your data or request the permanent deletion of your account and all associated personal data by emailing your request to **deekiiiiiii@gmail.com**.

Upon receipt and verification of your identity, your profile data, document uploads, and account histories will be permanently wiped from our active databases within a strict 30-day retention timeline.

## 6. Children and Youth Privacy

The Applications are intended for users aged 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from individuals under the age of 13. Individuals between the ages of 13 and 17 must not use the service without explicit parental or guardian consent.

## 7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update our Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in local legislation or operational upgrades. We will notify users of significant adjustments by altering the "Last Updated" date at the top of this document or via an explicit in-app banner announcement.
